Professional Profile

Based at Central Wyoming College, I specialize in the integration of pedagogical excellence with modern educational technology. My mission is to empower faculty and students by creating accessible, engaging, and innovative digital learning environments.

With a deep understanding of the unique challenges in rural higher education, I provide strategic support for the Canvas LMS, course accessibility compliance (ADA), and the development of multimedia instructional assets.

LMS Administration

Advanced support for Canvas, including course templates, integration of third-party tools (LTI), and faculty training workshops.

Digital Accessibility

Ensuring all course materials meet WCAG and ADA standards through proactive auditing and universal design for learning (UDL).

Instructional Media

Production of high-quality educational videos, interactive H5P content, and engaging visual aids to enhance course delivery.

Faculty Mentorship

One-on-one consultations to transform face-to-face curricula into effective online or hybrid learning experiences.

About Central Wyoming College

CWC is a community college serving Fremont, Hot Springs, and Teton counties, dedicated to empowering students through affordable academic and technical programs.
